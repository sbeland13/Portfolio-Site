Project Details

  • Feb 2016
  • MEAN stack (MongoDB, Express, NodeJS, Angular), Bootstrap, Heroku
  • Live Site

Basic application demonstrating MEAN (Mongo, Express, Angular, and Node) Integration with the Google Maps API. The application allows users to add themselves onto a map with identifying markers based on their form response. The application also demonstrates the use of HTML5 validated geolocation and the use of geospatial filtering through MongoDB.
