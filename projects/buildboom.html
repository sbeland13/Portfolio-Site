Project Details

  • Nov 2015
  • AngularJS, Firebase, SEO
  • Live Site

Buildboom is a social community startup for builders and contractors. The platform is built on an AngularJS front-end with Firebase back-end. I consulted with its developers to come up with the best solution to pre-render the site's content such that the major search engines are capable of reading and indexing the content on selected views.
