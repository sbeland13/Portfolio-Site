About Me

Stephane Beland

Following a decade-long career as a Process Engineer, my entry into the digital space began about four years ago with Web Design and Search Engine Optimization. As my skills expanded to include other forms of digital marketing and analytics, my interests moved pretty quickly to web development.

I started making custom websites, mostly using WordPress as a Content Management System, eventually moving on to building ecommerce sites and web applications, learning several new programming languages, REST APIs, LAMP and MEAN stacks along the way. I quickly fell in love with the JavaScript language and its wide range of frameworks and libraries.

With a solid base in UI development, I stay fluent, or at least conversational, in a slew JavaScript frameworks and libraries as they come and go: AngularJS, Meteor, Backbone.js, Node.js, React.js and so on. I am also growing quite fond of Ruby on Rails.

When I'm not working, you can find me renovating parts of my house, conquering a mountain, playing tug-of-war with my dog or enjoying the best local culinary spots with my wife.
